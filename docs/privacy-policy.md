# Privacy Policy for JargoNaut

**Last Updated: November 1, 2025**

## Introduction

JargoNaut ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how JargoNaut handles information when you use our browser extension.

## Information We Collect

**API Key Storage**
- JargoNaut stores your OpenRouter API key locally in your browser's storage
- This key never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us
- We have no access to your API key

**No User Data Collection**
- We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information
- We do NOT track your browsing activity
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking tools
- We do NOT store any history of posts you've analyzed

## Information Shared with Third Parties

**OpenRouter AI Service**
- When you use JargoNaut to explain a post, the post text is sent to OpenRouter's API
- This is necessary to generate the AI explanation
- OpenRouter processes this according to their privacy policy: https://openrouter.ai/privacy
- No identifying information about you is sent with the post text

## Data Storage

- Your API key is stored locally using your browser's built-in storage (Chrome Storage API / Safari Local Storage)
- All data remains on your device
- Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- You can clear your API key at any time through the extension popup
- You can uninstall the extension to remove all data
- You can request information about data practices by contacting us

## Children's Privacy

JargoNaut is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date.
